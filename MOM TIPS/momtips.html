💖 Welcome to Mom Tips 💖

Your guide to a **healthy, happy motherhood!**

💡 Motherhood Wisdom

🍼 Newborn Baby Care

👶 Keep skin-to-skin contact to bond with your newborn.
🛁 Give your baby a **gentle sponge bath** until the umbilical cord falls off.
🌡️ Always **test bathwater temperature** before putting the baby in.
🩺 **Regular check-ups** help monitor your baby's health.

🗣 Talking & Bonding with Your Baby

🎵 Sing lullabies & talk to your baby often – it helps **language development**.
👀 Maintain **eye contact** while speaking to build trust.
📖 Read simple storybooks even if your baby is **too young to understand**.

🌿 Mom’s Self-Care

💤 Sleep whenever your baby sleeps – rest is important for **your health**.
💧 Stay **hydrated** and eat nutritious meals to maintain energy.
🧘 Take **10 minutes daily** to breathe, stretch, or meditate.

🥗 Nutrition for Mom & Baby

🍏 Eat a **balanced diet** rich in iron, calcium, and vitamins.
🥕 Introduce solid foods at **6 months**, one ingredient at a time.
🍉 **Breastfeeding?** Stay hydrated & eat enough calories.

🧠 Mental Health & Emotional Well-being

❤️ **You are doing great!** Don't compare yourself to others.
📞 Talk to a **friend, partner, or therapist** if you're feeling overwhelmed.
🚶‍♀️ Daily walks help **reduce stress** & boost energy.
